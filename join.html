IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Mission Statement: "Camp Hazen YMCA is committed to helping youth develop valuable life skills through camping experiences that build healthy bodies, open minds and awakened spirits."

Physical Exam Required: State law and the American Camp Association accreditation requires that staff have a physical examination by a licensed Health Care Professional within two years of camp.

Personnel Policies: Camp Hazen YMCA is committed to providing a quality experience in a safe environment for the campers and staff. We ask staff to give up a great deal of privacy, the comforts of home, work long, demanding hours and adhere to policies which ensure an alcohol and tobacco free environment.

Staff Commitment: Working at Camp Hazen YMCA is an important responsibility. The rewards are found largely in the satisfaction of a job well done and in the personal growth and development of the campers and staff. Your submission of this form indicates your support of the Camp Hazen YMCA mission and your willingness to commit your best efforts and abilities towards achieving it.
